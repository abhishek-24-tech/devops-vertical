Our goal is to make contributions simple, transparent, and aligned with our culture of ownership and clarity.

# üìù Contribution Process (For Candidates)

## **1. Fork the Repository**
Click the **Fork** button on GitHub and create your copy of this repo.

Your fork is where you will add your assignment answers.

---

## **2. Create a Folder for Yourself**
Inside the root of your forked repository, create:

submissions/<your-full-name>/

Example:
submissions/Himanshu-Sharma/

This keeps all candidate submissions organized and traceable.

---

## **3. Add Your Assignment File**
Inside your folder, add:

assignment.md

Paste all your answers to **ASSIGNMENT.md** here.

You may use:
- Bullet points  
- Short paragraphs  
- Diagrams or sketches (optional)  
- Additional files if relevant (keep it simple)

---

## **4. Keep Your Submission Focused**
Do **not** modify:
- root-level files  
- public documents (VISION.md, ROADMAP.md, README.md)  
- other candidates‚Äô folders

Only work inside your own folder.

---

## **5. Commit With a Clear Message**
Example:

git add submissions/Himanshu-Sharma/assignment.md
git commit -m "Add Himanshu Sharma DevOps assignment submission"

We prefer clean, descriptive commit messages.

---

## **6. Open a Pull Request**
Go to **your fork ‚Üí Pull Requests ‚Üí New Pull Request**.

Set:

- **base repository:** ColoredCow/devops-vertical  
- **base branch:** main  
- **head repository:** your-fork  
- **head branch:** main  

Title your PR:

DevOps Assignment Submission ‚Äî <Your Name>

Example:

DevOps Assignment Submission ‚Äî Himanshu Sharma

---

## **7. Add a Short PR Description**

A simple format:

This PR contains my DevOps assignment submission.

What I‚Äôve included:

Answers to all sections in ASSIGNMENT.md

My thinking and approach across CI/CD, DevEx, and foundations

Questions I have for the team

---

## **8. Wait for Review**
We will:

- Acknowledge your submission  
- Review your answers  
- Ask clarification questions (if any)  
- Schedule a conversation with you  

Our review style is conversational ‚Äî not authoritative.  
You will always know what we‚Äôre thinking.

# üõ† Contribution Process (For External Contributors)

2. Create a new branch

- Public DevOps templates

- Learning resources  

Help us build a strong public DevOps practice.
